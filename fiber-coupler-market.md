<p><strong>The Fiber Coupler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Mode Couplers</li><li>Multimode Couplers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fiber coupler market comprises two primary types: single-mode couplers and multimode couplers. Single-mode couplers are designed for the transmission of signals through a single optical fiber core, enabling long-distance, high-bandwidth communication with minimal loss, making them ideal for telecommunication networks. Multimode couplers, on the other hand, facilitate the transmission of multiple light modes through a larger core, suited for short-distance applications like data centers and local networks, providing a cost-effective solution for high-capacity data transfer.</p></p>
