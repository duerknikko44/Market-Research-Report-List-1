<p><p>VPN services are categorized into two primary market types: Remote Access VPN and Site-to-Site VPN. Remote Access VPN allows individual users to securely connect to a private network over the internet, enabling secure access to resources from any location. In contrast, Site-to-Site VPN connects entire networks to each other, facilitating secure communications between multiple office locations or branches. Both types enhance security and privacy, catering to different needs in the increasingly interconnected digital landscape.</p></p>
