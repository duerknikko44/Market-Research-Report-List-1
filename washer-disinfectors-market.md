<p><p>The washer disinfectors market is primarily categorized into two types: cabinet (single chamber) machines and continuous process machines. Cabinet machines are designed for smaller healthcare settings, allowing for batch processing of instruments in a single chamber, ensuring thorough cleaning and disinfection. In contrast, continuous process machines provide a more efficient, high-capacity solution for larger facilities, enabling a constant flow of instruments through multiple stages of cleaning and disinfection, thus enhancing productivity and ensuring compliance with safety standards.</p></p>
