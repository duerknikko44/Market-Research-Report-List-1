<p><p>The FTIR fuel analyzer market incorporates various beam splitter technologies, primarily ZnSe-based and KBr-based beam splitters. ZnSe-based beam splitters are favored for their broad spectral range and durability, making them ideal for harsh environments. Conversely, KBr-based beam splitters are prized for their high transmission efficiency in the infrared range but are more susceptible to moisture and temperature fluctuations. Both types serve to enhance the accurate analysis of fuel components by facilitating effective infrared light splitting in spectroscopic applications.</p></p>
