<p><ul><li>Switching Power Supply</li><li>Converter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical power supply equipment market primarily includes switching power supplies and converters. Switching power supplies are efficient and compact, converting electrical power while minimizing heat generation, making them ideal for medical devices requiring steady voltage. In contrast, converters transform one form of electrical energy into another, such as DC to DC or AC to DC, ensuring compatibility with diverse medical equipment. Both types are critical for providing reliable, safe, and precise power needed in healthcare applications.</p></p>
