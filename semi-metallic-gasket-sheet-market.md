<p><p>The semi-metallic gasket sheet market is categorized into flat flange and non-planar flange types. Flat flange gaskets are designed for standard, flat surfaces, offering effective sealing in applications like pipelines and vessels. Non-planar flange gaskets are tailored for irregular or raised surfaces, accommodating complex geometries and ensuring a secure seal in demanding environments. Both types are crucial in industries like oil and gas, automotive, and power generation, where reliability and resistance to varied pressures and temperatures are essential.</p></p>
