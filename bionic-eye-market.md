<p><ul><li>Electronic</li><li>Mechanical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bionic eye market can be categorized into two main types: electronic and mechanical. Electronic bionic eyes utilize advanced technologies, such as microelectrode arrays, to directly stimulate retinal cells and restore vision for individuals with certain types of blindness. In contrast, mechanical bionic eyes focus on the use of assistive devices or prosthetics that emulate natural eye functions, aiding users in visual perception but requiring external control or support. Both types aim to enhance visual capabilities and improve quality of life.</p></p>
