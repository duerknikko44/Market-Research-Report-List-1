<p><strong>The Aquatic Herbicides Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Glyphosate</li><li>Imazapyr</li><li>Diquat</li><li>Triclopyr</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aquatic herbicides market encompasses various products designed to manage unwanted vegetation in water bodies. Glyphosate is a systemic herbicide effective against a broad spectrum of plants, while imazapyr acts as a non-selective herbicide, targeting specific aquatic species. Diquat, a contact herbicide, quickly disrupts plant cells, suitable for rapid control of weeds. Triclopyr primarily targets woody plants and is often used in aquatic environments for selective control. Each herbicide is formulated to address distinct challenges in maintaining aquatic ecosystems.</p></p>
