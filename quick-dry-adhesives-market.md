<p><p>The quick-dry adhesives market primarily consists of two types: cyanoacrylate and epoxy-based adhesives. Cyanoacrylate adhesives, known for their rapid bonding capabilities, cure quickly upon contact with moisture, making them ideal for small repairs and intricate applications. Epoxy-based adhesives, on the other hand, feature a two-part system that is versatile and offers high strength and durability but may take longer to set. Both types cater to diverse industries, including automotive, construction, and consumer products.</p></p>
