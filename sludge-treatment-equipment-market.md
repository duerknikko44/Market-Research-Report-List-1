<p><p>The sludge treatment equipment market comprises various technologies, including screw presses, centrifuges, and belt filter presses, each designed for efficient sludge dewatering and management. Screw presses utilize mechanical compression to reduce moisture content, while centrifuges employ centrifugal force for separation. Belt filter presses use a continuous belt system to filter sludge, enhancing sludge thickening. Other equipment types may include decanters, paddle dryers, or membrane systems, catering to diverse industrial needs for sludge treatment, enhancing waste management efficiency and sustainability.</p></p>
