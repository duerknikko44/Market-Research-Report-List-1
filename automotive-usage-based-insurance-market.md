<p><strong>The Automotive Usage-based Insurance Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pay-As-You-Drive(PAYD)</li><li>Pay-How-You-Drive(PHYD)</li><li>Manage-How-You-Drive(MHYD)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive usage-based insurance market includes three main types: </p><p>1. **Pay-As-You-Drive (PAYD)** bases premiums on the number of miles driven, rewarding lower mileage with lower rates. </p><p>2. **Pay-How-You-Drive (PHYD)** considers driving behaviors such as speed and braking patterns, encouraging safer driving through tailored premiums.</p><p>3. **Manage-How-You-Drive (MHYD)** offers feedback and tools to help drivers improve their habits, potentially lowering their insurance costs while promoting safer driving practices. Each type aims to incentivize responsible driving and reduce risk.</p></p>
