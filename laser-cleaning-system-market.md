<p><p>The laser cleaning system market comprises two primary types: gas lasers and solid lasers. Gas lasers, such as CO2 lasers, utilize gas as the lasing medium, offering efficient removal of contaminants like rust and paint, particularly on large surfaces. Solid lasers, on the other hand, use solid materials like crystal or glass for lasing, providing high precision and power, making them suitable for intricate tasks in various industries. Both types enhance effectiveness in cleaning applications across sectors like manufacturing and restoration.</p></p>
