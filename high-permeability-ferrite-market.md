<p><p>The high permeability ferrite market is primarily divided into two types: MnZn (Manganese-Zinc) and NiZn (Nickel-Zinc) ferrites. MnZn ferrites are known for their excellent magnetic properties and are commonly used in power transformers and inductors due to their high saturation magnetization and lower frequency applications. In contrast, NiZn ferrites exhibit higher resistivity and are ideal for high-frequency applications, such as in telecommunications and EMI suppression. Both types play crucial roles in various electronic components and devices.</p></p>
