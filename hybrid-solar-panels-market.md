<p><p>The hybrid solar panels market consists of two primary types: polycrystalline and monocrystalline hybrid solar panels. Polycrystalline hybrid panels are made from multiple silicon crystals, offering a cost-effective solution with moderate efficiency. In contrast, monocrystalline hybrid panels utilize a single crystal structure, providing higher efficiency and power output but at a premium price. Both types combine photovoltaic technology with additional features, enhancing energy production and sustainability, catering to diverse consumer needs and preferences in the renewable energy sector.</p></p>
