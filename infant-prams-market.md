<p><p>The infant pram market primarily includes two types: travel systems and conventional types. Conventional prams, on the other hand, are designed primarily for strolling and may feature a more traditional design without the car seat attachment. Each type serves specific needs, with travel systems focusing on convenience and versatility, while conventional types prioritize classic aesthetics and comfort.</p></p>
