<p><strong>The Deli Display Cases Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Forced Air Type Deli Display Cases</li><li>Gravity Coil Type Deli Display Cases</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The deli display cases market features two primary types: forced air and gravity coil. Forced air deli display cases utilize a fan system to circulate cool air, ensuring even temperature distribution for food preservation, making them suitable for high-traffic environments. In contrast, gravity coil deli display cases rely on natural convection, where cold air descends and displaces warmer air, providing a more energy-efficient solution. Both types are essential for maintaining food quality and presentation in delis and grocery stores.</p></p>
