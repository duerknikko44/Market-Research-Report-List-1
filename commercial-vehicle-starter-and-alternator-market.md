<p><p>The commercial vehicle starter and alternator market is divided into two main types: alternators and starter motors. Alternators generate electrical power to recharge the vehicle's battery and supply electricity to essential systems while the engine runs. Starter motors, on the other hand, are responsible for starting the engine by turning it over until combustion occurs. Both components are vital for the reliable operation of commercial vehicles, ensuring proper engine start-up and electrical system functionality during operation.</p></p>
