<p><ul><li>Oil-Injected Compressors</li><li>Oil-Free Compressors</li><li>Liquid Injection Compressors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Screw compressors are essential in industrial applications, and they come in three main types. Oil-injected compressors use a lubricant to enhance efficiency and cooling, making them suitable for various tasks. Oil-free compressors operate without lubrication, ensuring clean air output, ideal for sensitive applications like food processing and pharmaceuticals. Liquid injection compressors utilize a liquid (often water) to cool and lubricate while compressing the gas, offering high reliability and efficiency, particularly in high-pressure scenarios. Each type caters to specific operational needs.</p></p>
