<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Silicon Crystal Growing Furnace Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Czochralski(CZ) Method Furnace</li><li>Floating Zone(FZ) Method Furnace</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Silicon Crystal Growing Furnace market is categorized mainly into two methods: the Czochralski (CZ) method and the Floating Zone (FZ) method. The CZ method involves melting silicon in a crucible and slowly pulling a seed crystal to form a large ingot, widely used for semiconductor manufacturing. In contrast, the FZ method uses a localized melting zone without a crucible, allowing for higher purity crystals. Both methods cater to the growing demand for high-quality silicon in electronics and solar applications.</p></p>
