<p><p>The converter modules market is segmented into three main types: DC-DC, DC-AC, and AC-DC converter modules. DC-DC converters regulate voltage levels within DC systems, essential for powering electronic devices efficiently. DC-AC converters, or inverters, transform DC into AC power, commonly used in renewable energy applications like solar power systems. AC-DC converters convert alternating current into direct current, crucial for supplying power to electronic devices such as smartphones and laptops. Each module type serves distinct applications, influencing market dynamics and growth.</p></p>
