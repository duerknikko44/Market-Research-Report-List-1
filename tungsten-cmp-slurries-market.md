<p><p>Tungsten CMP (Chemical Mechanical Planarization) slurries are essential in semiconductor manufacturing for achieving smooth surfaces on tungsten films. The market for these slurries is divided into micron and nano types. Micron slurries, containing larger abrasive particles, are effective for initial polishing and removing excess material. In contrast, nano slurries utilize finer particles, providing superior control over surface roughness and enabling more precise finishes. The choice between these types depends on the specific requirements of the CMP process in semiconductor fabrication.</p></p>
