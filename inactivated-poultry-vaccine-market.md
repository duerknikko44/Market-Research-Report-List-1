<p><p>The inactivated poultry vaccine market comprises liquid and freeze-dried vaccines, both essential for immunizing poultry against various diseases. Liquid vaccines are ready-to-use formulations that offer convenience in administration but have a shorter shelf life. Freeze-dried vaccines, on the other hand, are lyophilized to enhance stability and extend storage life, requiring reconstitution before use. These two types enable poultry farmers to choose based on their storage capabilities, vaccine stability needs, and ease of administration in farm settings.</p></p>
